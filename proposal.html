The Racial Discrimination Issues Faced by Asians in the United States: Topic Proposal

Proposed Description

The issue of racial discrimination faced by Asians in the United States is a complex and serious topic. Despite advancements and successes in certain areas within the Asian community, they still frequently encounter various forms of discrimination and prejudice.

An information technology problem related to the issue of racial discrimination faced by Asians in the United States could be the lack of comprehensive data collection and analysis on discrimination incidents specifically targeting the Asian community.

One previous attempt to address the issue of racial discrimination faced by Asians in the United States is the development and use of online reporting platforms and apps specifically focused on hate crimes and discrimination incidents.
